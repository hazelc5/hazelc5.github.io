There are numerous activities to do while you are at the park. Here are some of my favorite things to do while at the park.

One of the activities you can do while your at the park is roller skate! I tend to go during the weekends when admission is free :)

Visit the pier 2 roller rink website to find the perfect day to roller skate!

If roller skating isn't your thing, you can enjoy a wonderful time riding Jane's Carousel by the Empire Fulton Ferry Section of the park.
